ORBES

A sonic journey that begins as a spiral and transforms into an orb: metallic, liquid, floating. Inspired by the vision of an object suspended between the tangible and the mysterious.

ABOUT

Leon Dubovsky is a producer blending organic textures and electronic experimentation. His music moves between introspection and rhythm, inspired by artists like Four Tet, Rival Consoles and Mogwai. Through a DIY approach, he creates soundscapes that are atmospheric yet physical, always searching for the balance between stillness and movement..
